Fourth Silver Sponsor: MySQL

June 6, 2015, midnight

We are happy to announce that MySQL will be a silver sponsor again for PyCon Singapore 2015. This is the second year in a row that they sponsor the conference and once again it is a unique opportunity for all attendees to meet MySQL staff at their booth during the conference.

"Oracle is shifting the complexity from IT, moving it out of the enterprise by engineering hardware and software to work together—in the cloud and in the data center. By eliminating complexity and simplifying IT, Oracle enables its customers—400,000 of them in more than 145 countries around the world—to accelerate innovation and create added value for their customers."

"MySQL is the world's most popular open source database. With its proven ease-of-use, performance, and scalability, MySQL has become the leading database choice for Web-based applications, used by high profile web properties including Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Yahoo!, and many more."
