Third Silver Sponsor: Thoughtworks

May 10, 2015, 7:23 p.m.

We are happy to announce that Thoughtworks will be a silver sponsor for PyCon Singapore 2015.

"ThoughtWorks is a custom software delivery and consulting global organization. It is home to passionate individuals who use technology to impact humanity through our clients with ambitious goals. With offices in 30 cities, across 12 countries, we want to revolutionize software delivery, while advocating for social change.

Our office in Singapore was established in 2012 with the mission to bring disruptive thinking and empowering technology to local organisations across all sectors. We build software solutions for the Lion City’s leading financial institutions and the government sector.

ThoughtWorks isn’t your average company and neither are our people. We set up smart teams who love challenges, enjoy exploring new domains and are open to using a variety of technology and the agile methodology to help our clients succeed.

Always Thinking: If you’re a technology enthusiast, then subscribe to the Insights feed."
