Announcing Pricing Tiers for PyCon Singapore 2015

March 8, 2015, midnight

We will open up early bird ticket sales very soon (at the end of this month) and since we got several emails already asking for this year's pricing, now is probably the right time to write about it:

We will offer 100 early bird tickets:

  • One tutorial: S$50
  • Conference ticket: S$180

Once the early bird tickets are sold out or we reach the beginning of May, we will offer our normal ticket prices:

  • One tutorial: S$70
  • Conference ticket: S$240

During the whole period, we will offer special student rates:

  • One tutorial: S$50
  • Conference ticket: S$80

You will be able to buy up to two tutorial tickets (one tutorial in the morning and one tutorial in the afternoon). Tutorials will be held on June 17th.

The conference ticket entitles you to attend both conference days, on June 18th and June 19th.

When we open up early bird sales, we will anounce it here in the news, on Twitter and via email to all users of https://pycon.sg

So if you are interested in attending the conference, please create an account at https://pycon.sg so that you don't miss your chance to get an early bird ticket.
