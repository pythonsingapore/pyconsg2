Meet Our Keynote Speakers

Andrew Godwin

Andrew is a Python programmer, Django core developer and the author of both the new Django migrations framework as well as South. He currently works at Eventbrite on the Architecture team, and when he's not working or out speaking at conferences he enjoys flying planes, archery, and exploring the world of cheese.

Lynn Root

An insomniac software engineer for Spotify, founder of the San Francisco Chapter of PyLadies, board member of the Python Software Foundation, and member of the Django Software Foundation. Lynn has a business degree in finance and economics, but fell in love with programming in 2011.

Jessica McKellar

Jessica McKellar is an entrepreneur, software engineer, and open source developer from San Francisco, California, USA. She is a Director for the Python Software Foundation, PyCon North America's Diversity Outreach Chair, and a maintainer for several open source projects, including OpenHatch and the Twisted event-driven networking engine. For her outreach efforts in the Python community, she was awarded the O'Reilly Open Source Award in 2013.
