Kenneth Reitz & David Cramer to be Keynote Speakers at PyCon Singapore 2014

March 22, 2014, midnight

That's right: No less than Kenneth Reitz and David Cramer will be so kind to take the long journey accross the ocean and honour us with their presence as keynote speakers for PyCon Singapore 2014!

Without any doubt, both of them are big names in the Python community and having them in Singapore in June will be an invaluable opportunity for our conference attendees to have interesting discussions and be inspired.

Kenneth Reitz

Kenneth Reitz is the product owner of Python at Heroku and a member of the Python Software Foundation. He embraces minimalism, elegant architecture, and simple interfaces. Kenneth focuses on software development, photography, and music production. He is well known for his many open source software projects, specifically Requests: HTTP for Humans.

David Cramer

David is the founder of Sentry (getsentry.com) and works in engineering at Dropbox. Previously he ran infrastructure at Disqus, which at the time was one of the highest trafficked Django applications. He's an active member of the open source community, passionate about scale, simplicity, and usability.
