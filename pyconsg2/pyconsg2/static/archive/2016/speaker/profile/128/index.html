Speaker profile of Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie

Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie

Delaney spent 7 years in bioinformatics research, and his background includes computer science, math, and statistics. He has been developing a quantitative finance curriculum for Quantopian using Jupyter Notebooks, and has worked with professors from schools including Princeton, MIT, Harvard, Cornell, and Stanford.
